Services

What we do

End-to-end computational biology solutions tailored to your research needs.

⚛

Molecular Dynamics Simulations

All-atom MD simulations of proteins, enzymes, and biomolecular systems including cellulose, lignin, and polymers. Conformational dynamics, substrate/inhibitor binding analysis, stability profiling, and structure-function characterization at atomic resolution.

♦

Protein Structure-Function Analysis

Investigating how sequence and structural changes affect protein function, stability, and interactions. Rational protein engineering guided by computational insights.

⚙

Metagenomics & Multi-Omics

Genome-resolved metagenomics, metatranscriptomics, metaproteomics, and metabolomics integration. From raw sequencing data to biological insights.

⚗

Resistome & AMR Profiling

Comprehensive antimicrobial resistance gene profiling, mobile genetic element tracking, and resistome characterization in environmental and clinical samples.

⚖

Bioinformatics Pipeline Development

Custom, reproducible, and scalable bioinformatics workflows for NGS data analysis, variant calling, functional annotation, and genome assembly.

✎

Scientific Writing Support

Manuscript preparation, figure design, statistical analysis, and data visualization for peer-reviewed publications in computational biology and related fields.
