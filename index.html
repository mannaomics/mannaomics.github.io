Services

What We Do

Specialized computational services spanning molecular, genomic, and systems scales, designed for research groups and industry teams who need rigorous, reproducible, and publication-ready outputs.

Molecular Dynamics Simulations

All-atom and coarse-grained MD simulations of proteins, enzymes, and complex biomolecular systems including industrial polymers. Services include conformational dynamics, free energy perturbation, substrate/inhibitor binding analysis, and structure-function characterization, integrated with AI-assisted structure prediction where applicable.

Protein Structure-Function Analysis

Computational investigation of how sequence variants, mutations, and structural perturbations affect protein function, stability, and molecular interactions. Combines homology modeling, AI-assisted structure prediction, and MD-based validation for rational protein engineering.

(Meta)genomics & Multi-Omics

Comprehensive multi-omics services including 16S/ITS amplicon profiling, whole-genome shotgun sequencing, (meta)transcriptomics, (meta)proteomics, and metabolomics. Core capabilities include genome assembly, MAG reconstruction, taxonomic and functional annotation, metabolic pathway reconstruction, and cross-omics network analysis. Data analyzed individually or integrated for systems-level biological interpretation.

Resistome & AMR Profiling

Comprehensive antimicrobial resistance gene profiling, mobile genetic element tracking, and resistome dynamics characterization across environmental, clinical, and agricultural samples. Applied within a One Health framework linking microbial ecology to public health.

Bioinformatics Pipeline Development

Development of custom, reproducible, and scalable bioinformatics workflows for NGS data processing, metagenomic assembly, functional annotation, and multi-omics integration. Built for HPC environments with documentation for long-term reproducibility.

Scientific Writing & Grant Support

End-to-end manuscript support, including results interpretation, statistical analysis, scientific figure preparation, and writing for peer-reviewed high-impact journals. We also assist with research grant proposal development, specific aims drafting, and computational methods sections for funding applications.

Training & Workshops

Hands-on training in bioinformatics, metagenomics analysis, R programming, and molecular simulation workflows. Designed for research groups, graduate students, and professionals from non-computational backgrounds. Available as one-day workshops or structured multi-session courses, delivered remotely or on-site.

Founder

The Scientist Behind the Science

Dr. Bharat Manna

Founder & Principal Scientist

Dr. Bharat Manna is a computational biologist with a Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur and postdoctoral training at the University of Auckland, New Zealand, where his research focused on the intersection of oxidative stress, microbial ecology, and antimicrobial resistance, a body of work increasingly relevant to global One Health challenges.

His expertise spans two scales: atomistic molecular dynamics simulations of protein systems, and genome-resolved multi-omics analysis of complex environmental microbiomes. This dual fluency, from force fields to metagenomes, is the foundation on which Manna Omics Solutions was built.

With 27+ peer-reviewed publications in journals including Water Research, ACS Sustainable Chemistry & Engineering, and Journal of Physical Chemistry B, and research presented across four continents, Dr. Manna brings both scientific depth and international perspective to every engagement.
